POPULATION EXPLOTIONS

The population explosion in Pakistan is seriously overburdening
the resources in health and education sectors.
According to some estimates, population of Karachi --provincial capital and financial hub of Pakistan has crossed over twenty four million.
The growing population poses a major challenge
to provincial administrating as the over congested city is already faced with issues such as huge traffic congestions,
sanitation and sewerage problems and water, electricity shortages.
This would not only reduce burden on the main city
but also help provide better facilities to people at their doorsteps.
